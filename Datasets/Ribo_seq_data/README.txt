Ribosome profiling data (Ribo_seq)

column1 Gene_id: AGI identifiers  in TAIR 10
column2	Chromosome:  Chromosome number
column3	Strand: strand information
column4	Position: 	position in choromosome
column5 Relative_distance: distance from 5' end of RNA
column6	AT01_adapter: read number in sample1
column7	Intron_flag: exon or intron
column8	AT02_adapter: read number in sample2
column9	Read_num: 	average read number in sample1 and sample2
column10 TPM: 	tags per million of mapped tags
column11 RO: 	TPM was normalized by RNA abundance
column12 From_start_codon: distance from first nucleotide of start codon
column13	From_stop_codon: distance from first nucleotide of stop codon
column14	Frame: 	translation frame
column15 Region: 5'UTR, CDS, 3'UTR or noncoding RNA
