Cleavage sites data (TREseq_seq)

column1 Gene_id: AGI identifiers  in TAIR 10
column2	Chromosome:  Chromosome number
column3	Position: Position in Chromosome
column4	Strand: strand information
column5	Relative_distance: 	distance from 5' end of RNA
column6 Intron_flag: OK is exon
column7	Read_num: number of reads
column8	TPM: tags per million of mapped tags
column9	CS: CSsite_values
column10	Region: 	5'UTR, CDS, 3'UTR or noncoding RNA
column11 From_start_codon: distance from first nucleotide of start codon
column12 From_stop_codon: 	distance from first nucleotide of stop codon
column14	Frame: translation frame
column15	Left: 	left side of nucleotide sequence 
column16 Right: right side of nucleotide sequence 
column17 C_Left	left side of codon sequence
column18 C_Right right side of codon sequence
column19 Flag TRUE = non-microRNA target
